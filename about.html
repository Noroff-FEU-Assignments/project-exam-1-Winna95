About Smoothie Bowls

Welcome! I'm happy you're here. My name is Winnie. I started this blog in 2023 and lives in Norway. I believe that healthy recipes are good for the body. I therefore started this blog to show that healthy food can be good.

I love healthy food. I love photographing food and hope my photos inspire you to step into the kitchen to try out my healthy delicious smoothie bowls. My recipes consist of organic and natural ingredients.

This blog is all about healthy smoothie bowls recipes and the recipes should help you feel good, avoid bloating and give your body what it needs. Hope you enjoy the page.
